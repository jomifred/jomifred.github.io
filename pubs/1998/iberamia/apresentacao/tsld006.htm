Agent’s problems in migration

  • On open societies, it maybe difficult for an entering to identify the roles of others directly through presentation because

    • the agent doesn’t have the skill to do so
      • it doesn’t know the language/protocol of the society it is entering
    • even if it knows the protocol, semantic problems may occur
      • For example: an agent that has the behaviour of taking products in one society may have its role represented there by the string “consumer”, and in the other by the string “client”
