How do we describe a role? Starting with process

  • To construct the role description we’ll use Hoare’s notion of process (communicating sequential processes,1985)

    • a process describes the behaviour of an object in terms of a sequence of events linked by operators (prefix, recursion, choice)

  • For example, let a consumer object behaviour be described by

      C =

      (request product
      ? receive
      ? (return ? C
      |
      consume ? C)
      ).
