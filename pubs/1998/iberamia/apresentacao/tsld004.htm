Open societies

  • Open societies bring new problems in role identification

  • They are societies where agents can enter or leave freely by movements called agent migrations

  • Migration may occur for two reasons

    • The agent may need some social functions that are only available in another society, forcing it to migrate in order to benefit from those functions
    • A society may need agent capabilities that can only be performed by an agent who resides in another society, the agent has to migrate for the society to benefit from the agent's capabilities
