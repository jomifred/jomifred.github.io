Identification of prefix

  • rewrite rule 1 (for general trace)

      <x>^s ? (x ? P) , s ? P

  • rewrite rule 2 (for empty trace)

      <> ? STOP

  • Example, let trace = <request product,receive>

      taking <request product> ^ <receive>

      by r1 ? (request product ? P1), <receive> ? P1
      taking <receive> ^ <>
      by r1 ? (receive ? P2), <> ? P2
      taking <>
      by r2 ? STOP
      Process = (request product ? receive ? STOP)

    Notation:
      x is an event

      s is a trace
      ^ is trace concatenation
      P is a process
