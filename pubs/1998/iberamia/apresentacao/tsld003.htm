Two role identification approach

  • Berthet, Demazeau and Boissier (Knowing each other better, 1992), propose two ways for acquiring knowledge about others:

    • Direct acquisition: an explicit request is executed
      • there is a presentation protocol where agents share information (role, skills, ....) among them.
    • Indirect acquisition: representation of the other is obtained by inferring knowledge from communication acts that have taken place.

  • The majority of works in MAS use direct acquisition.
